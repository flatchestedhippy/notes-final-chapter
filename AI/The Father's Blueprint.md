
### Mission
To build a life of freedom, presence and love so that my son can thrive while I'm here and continue to be supported with security and wisdom long after I'm gone.

### Vision
In 5–10 years, I am financially free - traveling, present, and deeply engaged in my son's life. I run a business that operates without me, generating wealth while I focus on what matters most: legacy, relationships, and peace of mind.

### Goals
- **Financial freedom through leverage** — Generate $50K+/month without trading time for money. Build systems, assets, and automated income streams that work while I rest.
- **Presence as the priority** — Structure life so that my son gets the best of me, not what's left of me.
- **Stress-free living** — Remove financial anxiety from daily life so that mental and emotional energy flows toward family and growth.
- **Legacy by design** — Make decisions today that protect and empower my son for decades to come, even in my absence.

### Principles
|Principle|What it means in practice|
|---|---|
|**Presence over productivity**|Time with my son is never sacrificed for busyness.|
|**Legacy & long-term thinking**|Every major decision is made with the next 20 years in mind.|
|**Simplicity & minimalism**|Eliminate what doesn't serve the mission. Complexity is the enemy.|
|**Integrity & trust**|I do what I say. My son will know who I was by how I lived.|

### AI System
#### Personal Assistant

A dedicated AI assistant that manages my daily operations — handling email communications, research, scheduling, and prioritization. It keeps me aligned with my Mission and Goals, surfaces what matters, and filters out what doesn't.

#### Agent Company

A always-on team of AI agents working 24/7 on my behalf across:

- **Email & communications** — drafting, responding, and triaging
- **Research & development** — market analysis, business intelligence, opportunity scouting
- **Business & investment analysis** — surfacing insights and tracking performance
- **Social media & content** — building presence and authority without demanding my time

Together, these agents act as a force multiplier — compounding my leverage while I stay focused on the things only I can do.

### How I'll know I've succeeded
- [ ]  Monthly income consistently exceeds **$50K** from systems that don't require my daily involvement
- [ ]  My son is **visibly thriving** — emotionally, physically, and in our relationship
- [ ]  I end most days feeling **calm, present, and unstressed** — not drained or reactive
- [ ]  A plan is in place to **support my son financially and with wisdom** beyond my lifetime
